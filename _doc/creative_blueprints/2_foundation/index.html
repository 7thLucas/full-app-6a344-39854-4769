The Case for Change

Problem → Solution

The Problem

Profit leaking in plain sight

  • ▸ Inventory tracked by memory or whiteboard — spoilage goes unnoticed until stems are unusable and the loss is final
  • ▸ 10–20% of purchased inventory spoils before use — a silent, weekly drain on an already thin margin
  • ▸ Orders managed in notebooks and group chats — specs get missed, arrangements go wrong, customers get disappointed
  • ▸ Delivery scheduling is verbal and ad hoc — conflicting instructions, missed windows, and damaged customer relationships
The Solution

One focused operations dashboard

  • ✓ Freshness-tracked inventory with 1–3 day alerts — match at-risk stems to open orders before they turn
  • ✓ Order management from intake to fulfillment — every spec, status, and due date in one place
  • ✓ Delivery scheduling with driver assignment and real-time status — from calendar to confirmed doorstep
  • ✓ Spoilage prevention dashboard — surface the risk every day before the loss becomes irreversible
