Foundation Validated

Manual inventory tracking and order chaos

The Problem

Florist owners lose revenue to wilted inventory, miss customer deadlines due to order chaos, and waste time manually tracking stock across multiple spreadsheets and order books. Without visibility into flower freshness and delivery windows, every day risks angry customers and discarded blooms.

The Solution

Florist shop owners struggle with inventory visibility, leading to flower spoilage, missed delivery deadlines, and lost revenue. Without a centralized system, managing stock levels, customer orders, and delivery schedules becomes chaotic and error-prone.

Target Audience

Independent florist shop owners and small floral design businesses with 1–3 locations

Personal Motivation

Help local florists scale operations, delight customers with punctual fresh deliveries, and reclaim time spent on manual admin work.
